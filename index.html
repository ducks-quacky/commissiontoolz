Available Commands

CommissionToolz offers a comprehensive set of commands to streamline your commission workflow. Here are the tools available to help you manage your art business:

/commission new

Create a new commission entry with client details, pricing, and deadlines.

/commission status

Update or check the current status of your active commissions.

/client add

Add new client information and contact details to your database.

/client history

View complete commission history for any client.

/pricing calculate

Calculate commission pricing based on complexity, size, and time estimates.

/schedule reminder

Set up automated reminders for commission deadlines and check-ins.

/invoice generate

Generate professional invoices for completed commissions.

/portfolio add

Add completed works to your portfolio with tags and categories.

/analytics report

Generate detailed reports on your commission income and productivity.

/backup create

Create secure backups of all your commission data and client information.
